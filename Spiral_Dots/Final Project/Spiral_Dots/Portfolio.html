Bootcamp 2021: Moving Dots

This is my first programming project, at the end of the two week Programming Bootcamp at City 2021. My project illustrates an understanding of the use of loops and sound processing. I have based my project on different coloured gradient dots moving in a circular motion with varying speeds where at the end they join together to make five circles each at different points on the canvas, with the smallest circle starting at the center. The five circles utilises 'for' statements each with different stroke colours to get the moving dots. This design is displayed on a background where the ellipses uses loops. Finally, I have added sound processing, on the visual that I have created to emphasise my project and to put into practice what I have learnt in the two weeks of bootcamp.
